To look at the reality in a unique point of view, creates the peak of madness.

Gündüz Vassaf

My father bought a green 74 model Wolksvagen when I was 16. As far as I can remember, our first trip was a month after it was bought. It was a father-son journey that lasted for 20 days.

To go somewhere from one place to another with a vehicle.

Sometimes without even knowing the destination.

Plenty of time.

A period that leads a person to think and to establish a dialogue.

Later in life, our paths parted with my father. We did not have the time to touch each other’s lives. However, during that trip, consciously or unconsciously he planted a seed inside me.

It took 4 years for it to bloom.

During that time period life got complicated.

The good, the bad, the pretty, the ugly, everything got tangled.

The solution of this complication was to set off.

These journeys were always to the north. Stillness of north made things easier.

Sometimes in order to see what is happening, I had to rise and look at myself from above.

Maybe that is why the direction was north.
