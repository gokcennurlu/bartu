I was born in Izmir, Turkey in 1993. I am studiyng in Radio, Television, and Cinema major at Marmara University. My passion for the art of photography kindled through my family trips during my childhood. I felt great pleasure from exploring the nature through a camera lens. After entering university, I obtained an analog camera, which transformed a machine that gave me visual and spiritual satisfaction to a device to be explored. As I grew older, my passion for travel and photography grew simultaneously. I worked as a barista to finance my journeys and traveled to 25 countries in Europe and Asia. The variety of cultural and geographical sceneries and the ways in which they blend and form something unique, formed a basis for my technique in photography as well as character.
